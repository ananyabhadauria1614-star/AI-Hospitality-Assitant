# 📂 02 — Pre-Arrival Workflow  
**Business function:** Guest Engagement & Operational Preparation  
**Trigger:** Booking confirmed  
**Prompts in this section:** P07  

---

## Section Purpose  

This workflow engages guests between booking and arrival, enabling proactive communication, modifications, and upselling. Without this step, hotels face increased last-minute requests and operational pressure. This system reduces check-in friction and improves preparation efficiency while increasing upsell opportunities.
