**Business function:** Feedback & Customer Retention  
**Trigger:** Guest checks out

## Section Purpose  

This workflow captures guest feedback and drives retention through personalised follow-ups. Traditional feedback systems have low engagement (often <20%). This prompt improves response rates and converts feedback into actionable insights and repeat bookings.
